Rock-Paper-Scissors

How-To-Play

Simply click on the input box, and type in your choice. Rock, Paper, or Scissors. Then, just click on the "Shoot!" button to let the computer know what your selection is. Afterwards, the winner is acccnouced on the lower center of the screen. Your chosen selection will be placed on the left, and the randomized computer selection will be placed on the right. Feel free to play as many games as you want. Enjoy!
